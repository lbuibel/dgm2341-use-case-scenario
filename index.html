Boxer 1200 Engine

Its powerful air-cooled, two-cylinder boxer engine features a capacity of 1170 cc and 110 hp of output for a highly dynamic power delivery, even at low speeds. Whether you’re in the city or on the country road, every journey is a special experience. The standard BMW Motorrad ABS and optional ASC (Automatic Stability Control) allow you to safely bring the unmistakable dynamic riding characteristics to the road. The upside-down forks are compression and rebound adjustable to suit your riding style. The newly designed tachometer readily informs you about everything you need to know, contributing to riding enjoyment.

Scrambler

Herritage

The R nineT has an uncomplicated style but nevertheless a completely unmistakable presence. It embodies the passion and innovation of over 90 years of motorcycle construction – naturally together with the classic BMW Motorrad proportions: striking boxer engine, compact tank, slim rear. All materials are of consistently high quality; the surfaces are carefully refined. Added to this is an immense attention to detail and real craftsmanship. The fender for instance is screwed to the fork feet using two forged brackets. And the headlamp holder is made out of aluminum.
